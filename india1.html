What to Expect

Language: Hindi is the most commonly used language in India. If you're travelling to major cities such as Delhi, Mumbai, English will not be a problem.

Currency: The currency used in India is Rupee. A pound is roughly equivilent to 90 rupee. An average meal in India should cost around 187 per person.

Recommendation: The most classic tourist destination is 'Golden Triangle' which includes Delhi, Jaipur and Agra is. For trekking and adventure, Himachal Pradesh is a great place to start.
